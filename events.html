About Hartron Education Institute

Empowering futures through high-quality, industry-relevant education and digital innovation.

1. Who We Are

Hartron Education Institute is a leading training and education provider established under the prestigious Haryana State Electronics Development Corporation Ltd. (HARTRON). Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and practical skills, offering specialized training programs in IT, electronics, and emerging technologies.

2. Our Vision

To be a premier institute in digital empowerment and skill development, nurturing a generation of tech-savvy professionals ready to lead the future.

3. Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver industry-aligned, accessible, and affordable education that empowers students with practical knowledge and prepares them for global career opportunities.

4. Courses We Offer

We provide a wide range of career-oriented programs including:

  • PGDCA – Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications
  • DCA – Diploma in Computer Applications
  • Web Designing & Development
  • Python Programming
  • Office Automation
  • Cyber Security Essentials

5. Facilities & Infrastructure

Hartron Education Institute provides a state-of-the-art learning environment with modern computer labs, smart classrooms, high-speed internet, and a digital library. Our faculty members are certified professionals with strong industry experience.

6. Why Choose Hartron?

Government Recognition: Backed by HARTRON, a government body, we ensure the highest educational standards.

Practical Learning: Our programs emphasize real-world applications, internships, and project work.

Placement Support: Dedicated career guidance and job placement assistance for every student.

7. Join Our Journey

Whether you're a student, professional, or job-seeker looking to upskill, Hartron Education Institute is your trusted partner in success. Discover your potential with our expert-led programs and begin your journey toward a brighter future.

Ready to start your journey with us? Contact us today or visit your nearest Hartron centre!
