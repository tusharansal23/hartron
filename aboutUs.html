Who We Are

HARTRON Skill Centre is an authorised computer education and training institute under the Haryana State Electronics Development Corporation Limited (HARTRON), a State Government undertaking. With over 25 years of excellence, we are committed to empowering individuals with the technical skills needed for success in the IT sector.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by offering affordable and high-quality IT education to the youth of Haryana. We aim to provide skilled manpower to the IT industry through NSQF-aligned certification programs and a progressive learning environment.

Government Recognition & Accreditation

HARTRON is aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF), ensuring nationwide recognition of our courses. Certifications issued are recorded in the National Qualifications Register (NQR), giving students broader acceptance in industry and academia.
